Privacy Guarantee
Your data never leaves your computer.

Byte Hoover processes all data locally in your web browser. This means:

  • No file uploads to external servers
  • No data storage or tracking
  • Complete privacy for sensitive information
  • You can even use it offline

The only network requests are for loading Bootstrap, Google Fonts, and JSZip.
